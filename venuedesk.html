Smart Booking Management

Take bookings online or walk-in. Automatic availability checking, room capacity enforcement and clash prevention — all in real time.

Customer CRM

Full customer profiles with booking history, payment records, notes and interaction logs. Know your regulars and give them great service.

✓ Complete customer view

Payments & Deposits

Collect full payments or deposits. Track outstanding balances, send automatic reminders and keep your accounts reconciled effortlessly.

✓ Full payment lifecycle

Enquiry Form
